So, what is the matter with the uses and effects of technology at school?

Published: Today | Category: Education | Author: John Smith

Technology has ushered in a new era of education. Online learning platforms have given students access to study materials, lectures, and other resources from anywhere with an internet connection. This is especially beneficial for those who live in remote areas or do not have access to physical classrooms.

Despite this, many teachers are still afraid of losing their jobs, as the internet and so-called AI (Artificial Intelligence) can teach students much faster and easier. However, machines are not capable of interacting like humans, who feel emotions such as affection, compassion, and love for teaching.

Recent studies show that while technology enhances access to information, the human element in education remains irreplaceable. The best results come from a balanced approach combining technological tools with traditional teaching methods.
